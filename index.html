FAQ

Still have
questions?

How soon will we see the first results? +

Most clients see significant engagement lifts and view count spikes within the first 30 to 60 days of launching their custom strategy. Building a sustainable, compounding and loyal audience typically takes 4 to 6 months of consistent, high-quality publishing.

Do you really produce all the content in-house? +

Yes, absolutely. We do not outsource your brand to freelancers. Our core in-house team handles every stage of your project: discovery & strategy, research, scripting, video recording, editing, sound design, thumbnail art, and channel distribution.

How much of our time does getting started take? +

We respect your busy schedule. Onboarding requires just a single 90-minute strategy session. From there, we handle research, script outlines, and logistics, needing only a 3-hour monthly filming day from you to shoot an entire month of ready-to-publish content.

Which businesses and creators is Marca right for? +

We specialize in founders, medical creators (doctors, clinics), consumer lifestyle brands, and high-end B2B businesses who want to leverage premium video production, branding, and graphic design to build authority and trust.
